All programs are fully accredited by the Kenya National Qualifications Authority (KNQA) and recognized internationally. Financial aid and scholarships are available for qualifying students.

Admission Requirements

  • 📋 Undergraduate Admission
    • KCSE Certificate with minimum C+ overall
    • Strong grades in relevant subjects
    • Two recommendation letters
    • Personal statement (500 words)
  • 🎓 Postgraduate Admission
    • Bachelor's degree (minimum 2nd Class Upper)
    • Academic transcripts
    • Research proposal (for PhD)
  • 🌍 International Students
    • Equivalent qualifications accepted
    • English proficiency (IELTS 6.0+)
    • Student visa requirements apply
  • 💰 Fees & Scholarships
    • Merit-based scholarships available
    • HELB loans accepted
    • Payment plans available
  • 📅 Application Deadlines
    • September intake: Apply by July 31
    • January intake: Apply by November 30
